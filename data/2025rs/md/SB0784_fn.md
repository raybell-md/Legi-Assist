Annual Corrective Bill 
 
 
This emergency, annual, corrective bill corrects errors and omissions in various articles of 
the Annotated Code and in uncodified laws. The bill also clarifies language, corrects 
obsolete references, reorganizes sections of the Annotated Code, and validates and ratifies 
corrections made by the publishers of the Annotated Code. The billâ€™s provisions are not 
intended to affect any law other than to correct technical errors. 
 
 
Fiscal Summary 
 
State Effect:  None. The bill is technical in nature and has no effect on governmental 
operations or finances. 
 
Local Effect:  None. 
 
Small Business Effect:  None. 
 
 
Additional Information 
 
Recent Prior Introductions:  Similar legislation has not been introduced within the last 
three years.
