Montgomery County Planning Board and Washington Suburban Sanitary 
Commission - Open Meetings - Live Streaming Requirement  
MC/PG 101-25 
 
 
This bill clarifies that a project site visit or an educational field tour does not constitute an 
open meeting for the Montgomery County Planning Board or the Washington Suburban 
Sanitary Commission (WSSC) if no organizational business is conducted; therefore, the 
requirement to stream live video or audio of specified open meetings is not applicable. The 
bill takes effect July 1, 2025. 
 
 
Fiscal Summary 
 
State Effect:  None. 
  
Local Effect:  The bill does not materially affect the operations or finances of the 
Montgomery County Planning Board or WSSC. 
 
Small Business Effect:  None. 
 
 
Analysis 
 
Current Law:  The Montgomery County Planning Board and WSSC are required to 
stream live video or live teleconference audio (or other audio) of open meetings, as 
specified. In addition, the Montgomery County Planning Board and WSSC must maintain 
on their respective websites a complete and unedited archived recording of each open 
meeting that is livestreamed.
