Correctional Services - Death of Incarcerated Individual - Notification 
 
 
This bill requires, whenever an incarcerated individual dies in a Division of 
Correction (DOC) facility, the Commissioner of Correction to notify (1) the State delegates 
and State senator representing the legislative district where the facility is located; (2) the 
legislative body of the county where the facility is located or the Baltimore City Council, 
if the facility is located in Baltimore City; and (3) the chief executive of the county where 
the facility is located or the Mayor of Baltimore City, if the facility is located in 
Baltimore City. The notification must include the cause of death of the 
deceased incarcerated individual and may not include protected health information of the 
deceased incarcerated individual. The Department of Public Safety and Correctional 
Services (DPSCS) must adopt regulations compliant with the federal Health Insurance 
Portability and Accountability Act (HIPPA) to implement the billâ€™s provisions. 
 
 
Fiscal Summary 
 
State Effect:  Potential minimal increase in general fund expenditures for DPSCS to 
provide the required notifications. Revenues are not affected. 
  
Local Effect:  None. 
 
Small Business Effect:  None.
