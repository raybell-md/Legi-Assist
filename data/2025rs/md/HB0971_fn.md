Anne Arundel County Crownsville Hospital Memorial Park Advisory Committee 
 
 
This bill establishes the Anne Arundel County Crownsville Hospital Memorial Park 
Advisory Committee. The committee must (1) act as an advisory body and provide advice 
on the planning process for Crownsville Hospital Memorial Park; (2) make 
recommendations regarding the development of the memorial park; (3) collect and compile 
public input on the development of the memorial park; and (4) make recommendations to 
ensure the memorial park is a place that focuses on specified goals for the park. By 
February 1 each year, the committee must report its achievements, findings, and 
recommendations to the Anne Arundel County Executive, the Anne Arundel County 
Council, and the Anne Arundel County Delegation. The bill terminates 
September 30, 2030.  
 
 
Fiscal Summary 
 
State Effect:  The bill does not materially affect State operations or finances.  
  
Local Effect:  The bill does not materially affect Anne Arundel County operations or 
finances.  
 
Small Business Effect:  None.  
 
 
Analysis 
 
Current Law:  Crownsville Hospital Center, located in Anne Arundel County, served as 
a State-run psychiatric hospital from 1911 until it was closed in 2004. The hospital was 
first established for African American patients and remained racially segregated until 1963.
