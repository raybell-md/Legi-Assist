Election Law - Gubernatorial Primary Election - Date 
 
 
This bill alters the date of the statewide primary election in the year in which the Governor 
is elected so that it is held on the fourth Tuesday in June, instead of the last Tuesday in 
June. 
 
 
Fiscal Summary 
 
State Effect:  The bill is not expected to materially affect State finances. 
  
Local Effect:  The bill is not expected to materially affect local government finances. 
 
Small Business Effect:  None. 
 
 
Analysis 
 
Current Law:  In the year in which the Governor is elected, the statewide primary election 
must be held on the last Tuesday in June. 
 
 
Additional Information 
 
Recent Prior Introductions:  Similar legislation has not been introduced within the last 
three years. 
 
Designated Cross File:  SB 645 (Senator Kagan) - Education, Energy, and the 
Environment.
