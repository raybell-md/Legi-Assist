A BILL ENTITLED  
 
AN ACT concerning  1 
 
Regulated Firearms – Seven–Day Waiting Period – Exceptions  2 
 
FOR the purpose of excepting a sale, rental, or transfer of a regulated firearm from a certain 3 
waiting period requirement if the purchaser, renter, or receiver of the firearm is a 4 
law enforcement officer, a retired law enforcement officer who retired in good 5 
standing, or an individual with a certain permit; and generally relating to 6 
purchasing, renting, or transferring a regulated firearm.  7
