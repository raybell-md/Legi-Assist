Environment  – Water – Individual Unit Meters  2 
 
FOR the purpose of  authorizing the installation of  individual unit water meters  for certain 3 
apartment houses and dwelling units; prohibiting the owner, operator, or manager 4 
of an apartment house, or a contractor hired by the owner, operator, or manager, 5 
from imposing certain costs on a unit; requiring each individual unit meter to inc lude 6 
a leak detection monitor; authorizing the occupant of a dwelling unit to periodically 7 
inspect the leak detection monitor installed for their unit; requiring the owner, 8 
operator, or manager of an apartment house, or a contractor hired by the owner, 9 
operator, or manager to maintain certain records; prohibiting unpaid water bills 10 
from being treated as unpaid rent in an eviction process; establishing a certain 11 
complaint process; and generally relating to individual unit water meters.  12
