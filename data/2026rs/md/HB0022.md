A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Correctional Services – Incarcerated Individuals – Identification Cards and
3 Driver’s Licenses

4 FOR the purpose of requiring a certain correctional facility to return a certain identification
5 card or driver’s license to a certain incarcerated individual at a certain time under
6 certain circumstances; requiring the Commissioner of Correction and the managing
7 official of a local correctional facility to provide certain forms to and work with a
8 certain incarcerated individual under certain circumstances to ensure a certain
9 identification card or driver’s license is available to the incarcerated individual at
10 the time of the incarcerated individual’s release from confinement; and generally
11 relating to incarcerated individuals.

12 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
13 Article – Correctional Services
14 Section 9–609.1
15 Annotated Code of Maryland
16 (2025 Replacement Volume)

18 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

19 Article – Correctional Services

20 9–609.1.

21 (a) (1) The Commissioner of Correction shall issue an identification card to an
22 incarcerated individual before release from confinement in a State correctional facility.
