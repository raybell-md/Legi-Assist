A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Charles County – Summer Academy for Mathematics and Service Pilot Program
3 – Establishment

4 FOR the purpose of establishing the Summer Academy for Mathematics and Service Pilot
5 Program within the Maryland Department of Labor to provide mathematics
6 instruction to certain students in Charles County; requiring the Charles County
7 public school system to implement the Program; requiring the Department to provide
8 grants to the Charles County public school system for the Program; and generally
9 relating to the establishment of the Summer Academy for Mathematics and Service
10 Pilot Program.

1 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
2 INDICATED.

3 (2) “ELIGIBLE STUDENT” HAS THE MEANING STATED IN § 11–608 OF
4 THE LABOR AND EMPLOYMENT ARTICLE.

5 (3) “PROGRAM” MEANS THE SUMMER ACADEMY FOR MATHEMATICS
6 AND SERVICE PILOT PROGRAM ESTABLISHED UNDER § 11–608 OF THE LABOR AND
7 EMPLOYMENT ARTICLE.

8 (B) THIS SECTION APPLIES ONLY IN CHARLES COUNTY.

9 (C) (1) IN PARTNERSHIP WITH THE MARYLAND DEPARTMENT OF LABOR,
10 THE CHARLES COUNTY PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM SHALL IMPLEMENT THE PROGRAM
11 TO PROVIDE TARGETED INSTRUCTION IN A 6–WEEK COURSE OFFERED DURING
12 SUMMER BREAK TO ELIGIBLE STUDENTS WHO ARE NOT PROFICIENT IN ALGEBRA OR
13 GEOMETRY.

14 (2) IN IMPLEMENTING THE PROGRAM UNDER PARAGRAPH (1) OF
15 THIS SUBSECTION, THE CHARLES COUNTY PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM SHALL:

16 (I) USE EVIDENCE–BASED MATHEMATICS INTERVENTIONS
17 AND ADAPTIVE LEARNING TECHNOLOGY;

18 (II) OFFER TRANSPORTATION CONSISTENT WITH
19 TRANSPORTATION AID GUIDELINES; AND

20 (III) OFFER MEAL ACCESS CONSISTENT WITH THE MARYLAND
21 MEALS FOR ACHIEVEMENT PROGRAM.

22 (D) THE CHARLES COUNTY PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM MAY PARTNER WITH:

23 (1) COMMUNITY COLLEGES OR UNIVERSITIES WITH TEACHER
24 PREPARATION PROGRAMS;

25 (2) PUBLIC LIBRARIES;

26 (3) STEM LEARNING CENTERS; OR

27 (4) NONPROFIT OR COMMUNITY–BASED ORGANIZATIONS WITH
28 DEMONSTRATED SUCCESS IN ACADEMIC TUTORING OR MATHEMATICS
29 ENRICHMENT.

11 Article – Labor and Employment

12 11–608.

13 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
14 INDICATED.

15 (2) “ELIGIBLE STUDENT” MEANS A STUDENT IN GRADES 9 THROUGH
16 11 WHO:

17 (I) SCORED BELOW A PROFICIENT LEVEL ON THE MOST
18 RECENT STATE ASSESSMENT IN MATHEMATICS; OR

19 (II) ATTENDS A PUBLIC SCHOOL IN WHICH FEWER THAN 90% OF
20 STUDENTS ACHIEVED A PROFICIENT LEVEL IN MATHEMATICS AS DETERMINED BY
21 THE MOST RECENT STATE ASSESSMENT.

22 (3) “PROGRAM” MEANS THE SUMMER ACADEMY FOR MATHEMATICS
23 AND SERVICE PILOT PROGRAM.

24 (B) THERE IS A SUMMER ACADEMY FOR MATHEMATICS AND SERVICE
25 PILOT PROGRAM WITHIN THE DEPARTMENT.

26 (C) THE PURPOSE OF THE PROGRAM IS TO PROVIDE MATHEMATICS
27 INSTRUCTION TO ELIGIBLE STUDENTS IN THE CHARLES COUNTY PUBLIC SCHOOL
28 SYSTEM TO MITIGATE THE EFFECTS OF LEARNING LOSS AND PROMOTE STUDENT
29 ACCELERATION.
