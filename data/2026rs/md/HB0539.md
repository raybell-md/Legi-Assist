2 (a) The State Board shall adopt rules and regulations for licensing and operating

4 (b) These rules and regulations shall:

5 (13) Subject to subsection [(c)] (D) of this section, establish probationary
6 employment qualifications for an individual who is applying for the first time to be a child
7 care teacher in a child care center in the State that serves preschool or school–age children
8 who are at least 3 years old.

9 (C) (1) THESE RULES AND REGULATIONS SHALL PROVIDE A PATHWAY
10 FOR AN INDIVIDUAL WHO IS QUALIFIED TO BE A CHILD CARE TEACHER, BUT LACKS
11 PROOF OF NECESSARY EDUCATIONAL ATTAINMENT, TO ATTEST TO THE QUALIFYING
12 EDUCATIONAL CREDENTIALS HELD BY THE INDIVIDUAL.

13 (2) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL ESTABLISH A PROCESS TO CARRY OUT
14 THIS SUBSECTION.

15 [(c)] (D) (1) The probationary employment qualifications established under
16 subsection (b)(13) of this section shall allow an individual to be employed as a child care
17 teacher during a probationary period if the individual:

18 (i) 1. Is enrolled in approved pre–service training;

19 2. Successfully completes the required 90 hours of the
20 approved pre–service training within 6 months after being hired; and

21 3. Holds an associate degree or a bachelor’s degree in:

22 A. Early childhood education;

23 B. Elementary education;

24 C. Child development;

25 D. Home economics;

26 E. Nursing;

27 F. Social work;

28 G. Special education; or

29 H. A related field approved by the Department; or
