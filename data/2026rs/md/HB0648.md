A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Office of Home Energy Programs – Uniform Redetermination Process – Age

3 FOR the purpose of reducing the age at which certain eligible energy customers qualify for
4 a certain redetermination process for enrollment in energy assistance programs; and
5 generally relating to the Office of Home Energy Programs and energy assistance
6 programs.

7 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
8 Article – Human Services
9 Section 5–5A–09(a)
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2019 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

12 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
13 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

14 Article – Human Services

15 5–5A–09.

16 (a) (1) The Office shall develop a uniform redetermination process to assist
17 eligible energy customers who are at least [65] 60 years old in enrolling in energy
18 assistance programs.

19 (2) The redetermination process developed under paragraph (1) of this
20 subsection shall be updated annually.

21 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
22 1, 2026.
