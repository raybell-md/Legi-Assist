A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Public Safety – Law Enforcement Agencies – Use of Body–Worn Cameras

3 FOR the purpose of altering certain provisions of law to require a law enforcement agency
4 to require the use of body–worn cameras, subject to a certain policy, by each sworn
5 law enforcement officer, regardless of rank, employed by the law enforcement agency
6 while the officer is in uniform, in public, and conducting law enforcement–related
7 duties; and generally relating to the use of body–worn cameras by law enforcement
8 officers.
