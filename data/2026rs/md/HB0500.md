A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Sales and Use Tax – Precious Metal Bullion or Coins – Exemption

3 FOR the purpose of altering the definition of “precious metal bullion or coins” for purposes
4 of eligibility for an exemption from the sales and use tax for the sale of precious metal
5 bullion or coins; expanding the exemption by repealing requirements that the sale
6 price exceed a certain amount and the sale occur at the Baltimore Convention
7 Center; and generally relating to a sales and use tax exemption for precious metal
8 bullion and coins.

9 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
10 Article – Tax – General
11 Section 11–214.1
12 Annotated Code of Maryland

19 (1) “precious metal bullion or coins” means:
