A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Peer–to–Peer Car Sharing Programs – Insurance and Liability

3 FOR the purpose of repealing certain provisions of law authorizing a peer–to–peer car
4 sharing program’s motor vehicle liability insurance policy on a replacement vehicle
5 to be secondary coverage for a shared vehicle driver; altering and establishing certain
6 motor vehicle liability insurance requirements applicable to peer–to–peer car
7 sharing programs; providing that the Maryland Automobile Insurance Fund is not
8 required to provide coverage to a shared vehicle drive for the use of a shared vehicle
9 that is not a replacement vehicle; repealing the prohibition on certain insurers
10 taking certain actions with respect to a personal motor vehicle liability insurance
11 policy of a shared vehicle owner solely on the basis that a certain vehicle has been
12 made available for sharing through a peer–to–peer car sharing program except
13 under certain circumstances; providing that certain provisions of law do not
14 invalidate or limit exclusions contained in a motor vehicle liability insurance policy;
15 authorizing a peer–to–peer car sharing program to transfer monetary liability and
16 charge a shared vehicle driver for certain tolls, fees, charges, or fines imposed under
17 certain circumstances; excluding a peer–to–peer car sharing program from the
18 definition of “owner” for the purpose of citations issued for violations recorded by a
19 traffic control signal monitoring system; and generally relating to peer–to–peer car
20 sharing programs.
