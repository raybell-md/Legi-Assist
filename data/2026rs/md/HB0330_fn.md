Maryland Legal Services Corporation - Board of Directors - Membership 
 
 
This bill increases the number of members on the Board of Directors of the Maryland Legal 
Services Corporation (MLSC) and makes related changes. Specifically, the bill increases 
(1) from 9 to 12, the number of voting members; (2) from 5 to 7, the number of voting 
members who must be lawyers admitted to the Bar in the State; (3) from 4 to 5, the voting 
members who must be nonlawyers; and (4) from 7 to 9, the number of voting members 
required to remove a voting member. 
 
 
Fiscal Summary 
 
State Effect:  None. Altering the composition of the board does not affect State operations 
or finances. 
  
Local Effect:  None. 
  
Small Business Effect:  None.
