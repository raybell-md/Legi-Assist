A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Maryland Heritage Area Authority and Heritage Areas – Alterations

3 FOR the purpose of altering the voting membership of the Maryland Heritage Area
4 Authority; increasing the number of voting members of the Authority that
5 constitutes a quorum; repealing the requirement that the Authority send copies of
6 boundary maps to certain county clerks; repealing the requirement that certain
7 State officials develop certain program statements and instead requiring certain
8 State officials and certain units of State government to support and assist certain
9 activities in certified heritage areas in certain ways; authorizing the Authority to
10 award certain grants and loans for certain purposes in a certain manner; repealing
11 certain requirements for certain grants and loans; and generally relating to the
12 Maryland Heritage Area Authority and heritage areas.
