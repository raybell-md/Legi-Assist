2 Vehicle Laws – Ignition Interlock System Program – Required Participation

3 FOR the purpose of requiring an individual to be a participant in the Ignition Interlock
4 System Program as a condition of modification of a suspension or revocation of a
5 license or issuance of a restricted license if the individual violated certain alcohol
6 restrictions; and generally relating to required participation in the Ignition Interlock
7 System Program.

17 (i) A service provider who applies to the Administration for

19 able to competently service, install, monitor, calibrate, and provide information to the
20 Administration at least every 30 days on individuals required to use ignition interlock
21 systems;

22 (ii) A service provider who applies to the Administration for
23 certification as an approved service provider shall be certified by a signed affidavit from
24 the manufacturer that the service provider has been trained by an authorized
25 manufacturer and that the service provider is competent to service, install, monitor,
26 calibrate, and provide information on ignition interlock systems;

27 (iii) Approved service providers be deemed to be authorized
28 representatives of a manufacturer; and

29 (iv) Any service of notice upon an approved service provider, who has
30 violated any laws or regulations or whose ignition interlock system has violated any laws
31 or regulations, be deemed as service upon the manufacturer who certified the approved
32 service provider.
