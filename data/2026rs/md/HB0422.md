A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Public Health – Expedited Partner Therapy – Bacterial Vaginosis

3 FOR the purpose of adding bacterial vaginosis to the list of diagnoses for which certain
4 health care providers may prescribe, dispense, or otherwise provide antibiotic
5 therapy to a sexual partner of a diagnosed patient without making a certain
6 assessment of the sexual partner; and generally relating to expedited partner
7 therapy.

8 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
9 Article – Health – General
10 Section 18–214.1
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2023 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

15 Article – Health – General

16 18–214.1.

17 (a) The purpose of expedited partner therapy is to provide antibiotic therapy to
18 any partner of a patient diagnosed with a sexually transmitted infection identified in
19 subsection (b) of this section in order to:

20 (1) Contain and stop the further spread of the infection; and

21 (2) Reduce the likelihood of reinfection in the diagnosed patient.
