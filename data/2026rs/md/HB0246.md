A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Video Tolls – Class G (Trailer) Vehicles – Liability

3 FOR the purpose of establishing that the registered owner of a Class G (trailer) vehicle is
4 liable to the Maryland Transportation Authority for payment of a video toll assessed at
5 the time of passage through a toll collection facility, subject to certain standards and
6 procedures for the transfer of liability; and generally relating to liability for the
7 payment of video tolls.
