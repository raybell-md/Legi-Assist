6 (A) A CHILD, INCLUDING ONE SUBJECT TO ADULT CRIMINAL COURT
7 JURISDICTION, WHO HAS BEEN ARRESTED, HAS BEEN CONVICTED, OR IS AWAITING
8 TRIAL ON CRIMINAL CHARGES MAY NOT BE DETAINED OR CONFINED IN ANY
9 INSTITUTION IN WHICH THE CHILD HAS CONTACT WITH OR COMES WITHIN SIGHT OR
10 SOUND OF AN INCARCERATED ADULT.

11 (B) EXCEPT AS PROVIDED IN SUBSECTION (C) OF THIS SECTION, A CHILD
12 WHO HAS BEEN ARRESTED, HAS BEEN CONVICTED, OR IS AWAITING TRIAL FOR AN
13 OFFENSE UNDER ADULT CRIMINAL COURT JURISDICTION MAY NOT BE HELD IN
14 CUSTODY IN AN ADULT CORRECTIONAL FACILITY.

15 (C) A CHILD MAY BE TEMPORARILY HELD FOR PROCESSING IN AN ADULT
16 JAIL OR A CORRECTIONAL OR DETENTION FACILITY THAT DOES NOT HAVE A SECURE
17 JUVENILE DETENTION AREA IF THE CHILD IS:

18 (1) SEPARATED BY SIGHT AND SOUND FROM INCARCERATED ADULTS;
19 AND

20 (2) HELD FOR A PERIOD NOT EXCEEDING 6 HOURS, INCLUDING TIME
21 IN THE FACILITY AND IN TRANSPORT TO THE NEAREST JUVENILE FACILITY.

22 [(c)] (D) A child may not be transported together with adults who have been
23 charged with or convicted of a crime [unless the court has waived its jurisdiction and the
24 child is being proceeded against as an adult].
