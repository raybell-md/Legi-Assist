A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Baltimore City – Alcoholic Beverages – License Extension

3 FOR the purpose of extending for a certain amount of time the expiration date of an
4 alcoholic beverages license issued for a certain area of Baltimore City for the purpose
5 of completing renovations; and generally relating to alcoholic beverages licenses in
6 Baltimore City.

7 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
8 That, notwithstanding § 12–2202 of the Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis Article, a
9 racetrack license issued for a premises in the 5200 block of Park Heights Avenue shall be
10 considered unexpired until the end of July 1, 2029, for the purpose of completing
11 renovations at the same location.

12 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
13 1, 2026. It shall remain effective for a period of 3 years and 1 month and, at the end of July
14 31, 2029, this Act, with no further action required by the General Assembly, shall be
15 abrogated and of no further force and effect.
