A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Education – Maryland Center for School Safety – Anonymous Reporting System

3 FOR the purpose of requiring the Maryland Center for School Safety to develop and
4 administer an anonymous reporting system; requiring the Center to develop
5 operational guidelines and procedures for the anonymous reporting system and to
6 provide certain training; requiring each local school system to participate in the
7 anonymous reporting system, subject to a certain exception; requiring a certain
8 custodian of records to deny inspection of information and materials related to the
9 anonymous reporting system; and generally relating to the school safety anonymous
10 reporting system.

17 (A) (1) THE CENTER SHALL DEVELOP AND ADMINISTER AN ANONYMOUS
18 REPORTING SYSTEM.

19 (2) THE ANONYMOUS REPORTING SYSTEM SHALL:

20 (I) COORDINATE THE RECEIPT OF INFORMATION RELATING TO
21 A SCHOOL OR STUDENT SAFETY CONCERN IN A MANNER THAT MAINTAINS THE
22 ANONYMITY OF THE INDIVIDUAL REPORTING THE INFORMATION; AND

23 (II) PROVIDE A MECHANISM FOR ANY REPORTED INFORMATION
24 TO BE FORWARDED TO SCHOOL OFFICIALS OR OTHER APPROPRIATE RESPONSE
25 PERSONNEL.

26 (3) THE CENTER SHALL:

27 (I) DEVELOP OPERATIONAL GUIDELINES AND PROCEDURES
28 FOR THE ANONYMOUS REPORTING SYSTEM; AND

29 (II) PROVIDE TRAINING SESSIONS FOR SCHOOL PERSONNEL ON
30 NAVIGATING THE ANONYMOUS REPORTING SYSTEM.
