A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Correctional Officers’ Retirement System – Redeposit of Accumulated
3 Contributions – Alterations

4 FOR the purpose of authorizing a member of the Correctional Officers’ Retirement System
5 to receive certain service credit on the redeposit of contributions from the Employees’
6 Pension System or the Teachers’ Pension System; and generally relating to service
7 credit in the Correctional Officers’ Retirement System.

8 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
9 Article – State Personnel and Pensions
10 Section 25–304
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2024 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

15 Article – State Personnel and Pensions

16 25–304.

17 A member of the Correctional Officers’ Retirement System who previously
18 terminated membership and withdrew accumulated contributions from either the
19 Correctional Officers’ Retirement System, the Employees’ Retirement System, [or] THE
20 EMPLOYEES’ PENSION SYSTEM, the Teachers’ Retirement System, OR THE TEACHERS’
21 PENSION SYSTEM may receive service credit for the period of employment during the
22 previous membership if the member redeposits in a single payment the withdrawn
23 accumulated contributions plus regular interest to the date of redeposit.
