1 AN ACT concerning

2 Election Law – Affiliating With a Party and Voting – Unaffiliated Voters

3 FOR the purpose of authorizing unaffiliated voters to request to affiliate with a political
4 party at an early voting center during a primary election and vote a provisional ballot
5 for the voter’s preferred political party; and generally relating to affiliating with a
6 political party and voting in a primary election.
