A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Criminal Law – Nondomesticated Animal Prohibitions – Alterations

3 FOR the purpose of altering a certain exception to the prohibition against importing,
4 offering for sale, trading, bartering, possessing, breeding, or exchanging certain live,
5 nondomesticated animals and repealing a certain other exception; prohibiting
6 certain live nondomesticated animals from being imported, offered for sale, traded,
7 bartered, possessed, bred, or exchanged on or after a certain date, subject to a certain
8 exception; and generally relating to the prohibition against the importation, sale,
9 trade, barter, possession, breeding, or exchange of certain nondomesticated animals.

10 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
11 Article – Criminal Law
12 Section 10–621(a) and (b)(1)
