HOUSE BILL 384

D4, D1 6lr1914

By: Delegates Taveras, Fennell, Hill, Kaiser, Kaufman, Lehman, Moreno, Odom,
Ruff, Stinnett, Terrasa, and Wims
Introduced and read first time: January 21, 2026
Assigned to: Judiciary

A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Child Support – Capacity of Minors to Initiate and Defend an Action

3 FOR the purpose of authorizing a minor who is the parent of a child to initiate or defend
4 an action for child support; and generally relating to child support.

5 BY adding to
6 Article – Family Law
7 Section 12–106
8 Annotated Code of Marylan
9 (2019 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

10 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
11 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

12 Article – Family Law

13 12–106.

14 NOTWITHSTANDING MARYLAND RULE 2–202, A MINOR WHO IS THE PARENT
15 OF A CHILD HAS THE CAPACITY AND RIGHT TO INITIATE OR DEFEND AN ACTION FOR
16 CHILD SUPPORT, WHETHER PENDENTE LITE OR PERMANENTLY.

17 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
18 October 1, 2026.
