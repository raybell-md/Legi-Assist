A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Environment – Synthetic Turf Industry and Disposal Study – Deadline
3 Extension

4 FOR the purpose of extending the deadline by which the Department of the Environment
5 is required to submit a certain report on the synthetic turf industry and synthetic
6 turf disposal; and generally relating to synthetic turf.

7 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
8 Chapter 567 of the Acts of the General Assembly of 2024
9 Section 2

10 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
11 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

12 Chapter 567 of the Acts of 2024

13 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That:

14 (a) The Department of the Environment, in consultation with the Synthetic Turf
15 Council and other representatives of the synthetic turf industry, local environmental and
16 community stakeholders, and synthetic turf producers, retailers, and users, including local
17 governments, county school systems, higher education facilities, and professional sports
18 organizations, shall conduct a study on:

19 (1) synthetic turf;

20 (2) existing synthetic turf fields in the State; and

21 (3) the synthetic turf industry in the State.
