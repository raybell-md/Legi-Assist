1 (c) (1) (i) It is the goal of the State that students enrolled in public school
2 shall meet the CCR standard before the end of the 10th grade and no later than the time
3 the student graduates from high school.

4 (ii) It is the goal of the State that each student enrolled in public
5 school, regardless of the student’s race, ethnicity, gender, address, socioeconomic status, or
6 the language spoken in the student’s home, shall have equitable access to college and career
7 readiness and shall meet the CCR standard at an equal rate.

8 (2) A student shall meet the CCR standard when the student meets a
9 standard in English language arts, mathematics, and, when practicable, science that
10 enables the student to be successful in entry level credit bearing courses or postsecondary
11 education training at a State community college.

12 (3) The Blueprint for Maryland’s Future and the CCR standard established
13 under this section are not intended to alter the need for high quality programs and content
14 in fine arts, civics, physical education, and other areas that are necessary to provide a
15 holistic education and enable every student to be well–rounded and meet the CCR
16 standard.

17 (4) On or before January 1, 2022, the Department shall develop and begin
18 to implement a communication strategy to inform parents, students, educators, and the
19 wider public about the CCR standard developed under this section.
