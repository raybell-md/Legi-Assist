A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Correctional Services – Immigration Enforcement – Required Notice and
3 Transfer

4 FOR the purpose of requiring an employee or agent of a State or local correctional facility
5 to provide certain notice of the release of an individual and transfer the individual
6 to federal immigration authorities under certain circumstances; and generally
7 relating to immigration enforcement.

8 BY adding to
9 Article – Correctional Services
10 Section 9–309
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2025 Replacement Volume)

13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

15 Article – Correctional Services

16 9–309.

17 IF REQUESTED BY FEDERAL IMMIGRATION AUTHORITIES, AN EMPLOYEE OR
18 AGENT OF A STATE OR LOCAL CORRECTIONAL FACILITY SHALL:

19 (1) PROVIDE NOTICE OF THE RELEASE OF AN INDIVIDUAL AT LEAST
20 48 HOURS PRIOR TO THE RELEASE OF THE INDIVIDUAL IN ORDER TO FACILITATE
21 THE TRANSFER OF THE INDIVIDUAL TO FEDERAL IMMIGRATION AUTHORITIES; AND
