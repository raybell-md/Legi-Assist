3 (3) (I) “ELECTRONIC COMMUNICATION DEVICE” MEANS ANY
4 PORTABLE ELECTRONIC DEVICE CARRIED OR TRANSPORTED BY A STUDENT TO
5 RECEIVE, SEND, OR RECORD VOICE, IMAGE, OR TEXT CONTENT, INCLUDING CELL
6 PHONES, TABLETS, LAPTOPS, SMART WATCHES, WIRELESS EARBUDS OR
7 HEADPHONES, GEOLOCATION DEVICES, AND ANY OTHER DEVICE WITH
8 COMMUNICATION FUNCTIONS.

9 (II) “ELECTRONIC COMMUNICATION DEVICE” DOES NOT
10 INCLUDE AN ELECTRONIC COMMUNICATION DEVICE PROVIDED BY A COUNTY BOARD
11 FOR INSTRUCTIONAL PURPOSES.

12 (4) “STORE” MEANS TO SECURE IN A MANNER DETERMINED BY THE
13 COUNTY BOARD TO ENSURE THE ELECTRONIC COMMUNICATION DEVICE IS NOT
14 CARRIED ON THE STUDENT’S PERSON DURING THE ACADEMIC SCHOOL DAY.

15 (B) NOT LATER THAN THE 2027–2028 SCHOOL YEAR, EACH COUNTY BOARD
16 SHALL DEVELOP AND IMPLEMENT A POLICY TO LIMIT THE USE OF ELECTRONIC
17 COMMUNICATION DEVICES BY STUDENTS DURING THE ACADEMIC SCHOOL DAY.

18 (C) EXCEPT AS PROVIDED IN SUBSECTION (D) OF THIS SECTION, THE
19 POLICY DEVELOPED UNDER THIS SECTION SHALL:

20 (1) PROHIBIT A STUDENT FROM USING AN ELECTRONIC
21 COMMUNICATION DEVICE DURING THE ACADEMIC SCHOOL DAY;

22 (2) REQUIRE A STUDENT TO STORE THE STUDENT’S ELECTRONIC
23 COMMUNICATION DEVICE DURING THE ACADEMIC SCHOOL DAY;

24 (3) PROHIBIT A STUDENT FROM USING THE SOCIAL MEDIA
25 APPLICATIONS AND SOCIAL MEDIA WEBSITES DETERMINED BY THE COUNTY BOARD
26 DURING THE ACADEMIC SCHOOL DAY; AND

27 (4) REQUIRE ADMINISTRATOR APPROVAL FOR THE USE OF A
28 PERSONAL ELECTRONIC COMMUNICATION DEVICE FOR EDUCATIONAL PURPOSES
29 WHEN A SCHOOL–ISSUED ELECTRONIC COMMUNICATION DEVICE IS UNAVAILABLE.

30 (D) THE POLICY DEVELOPED UNDER THIS SECTION MAY NOT PROHIBIT A
31 STUDENT FROM USING AN ELECTRONIC COMMUNICATION DEVICE:
