2 means a percentage, rounded to the nearest two decimal places, that is the lesser of:

3 (i) The increase in the implicit price deflator for State and local

5 (ii) The Consumer Price Index for All Urban Consumers for the
6 Washington Metropolitan Area, or any successor index, for the second prior fiscal year; or

7 (iii) 5%.

8 (2) If there is no increase in the implicit price deflator for State and local
9 government expenditures for the second prior fiscal year or in the Consumer Price Index
10 for All Urban Consumers for the Washington Metropolitan Area, or any successor index,
11 for the second prior fiscal year, then “inflation adjustment” means 0%.

15 to provide an amount of revenue for elementary and secondary public education purposes
16 equal to the local share of major education aid as adjusted under § 5–239 of this subtitle.

22 1. State appropriations;
