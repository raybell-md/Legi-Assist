A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Motor Vehicles – Parking – Disabled Veteran Special Registration Plates

3 FOR the purpose of prohibiting a political subdivision from adopting or enforcing an
4 ordinance, a regulation, or a policy that requires payment of a fee for parking a motor
5 vehicle in a parking space served by a parking meter if the motor vehicle has disabled
6 veteran special registration plates; prohibiting a State agency from adopting or
7 enforcing a regulation or a policy that requires payment of a fee for parking a motor
8 vehicle in a parking lot owned or controlled by the State agency if the motor vehicle
9 has disabled veteran special registration plates; and generally relating to parking by
10 motor vehicles that have disabled veteran special registration plates.

1 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
2 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

3 Article – Transportation

4 26–301.

5 (b) Subject to [subsection (c)] SUBSECTIONS (C) AND (C–1) of this section, any
6 State agency authorized by law and any political subdivision of this State may adopt
7 ordinances or regulations that:

8 (1) Regulate the parking of vehicles;

9 (2) Provide for the impounding of vehicles parked in violation of the
10 ordinances or regulations;

11 (3) Regulate the towing of vehicles from publicly owned and privately
12 owned parking lots; and

13 (4) Provide for the issuance of a citation by an officer for a violation of an
14 ordinance or regulation that is adopted under this section.

15 (c) A political subdivision may not adopt or enforce an ordinance or regulation
16 that prohibits the parking of more than one motorcycle within a space served by a single
17 parking meter.

18 (C–1) (1) A POLITICAL SUBDIVISION MAY NOT ADOPT OR ENFORCE AN
19 ORDINANCE, A REGULATION, OR A POLICY THAT REQUIRES PAYMENT OF A FEE FOR
20 PARKING A MOTOR VEHICLE IN AN ON–STREET PARKING SPACE SERVED BY A
21 PARKING METER IF THE MOTOR VEHICLE HAS DISABLED VETERAN SPECIAL
22 REGISTRATION PLATES ISSUED BY THE ADMINISTRATION.

23 (2) A STATE AGENCY MAY NOT ADOPT OR ENFORCE A REGULATION
24 OR A POLICY THAT REQUIRES PAYMENT OF A FEE FOR PARKING A MOTOR VEHICLE
25 IN A PARKING LOT OWNED OR CONTROLLED BY THE STATE AGENCY IF THE MOTOR
26 VEHICLE HAS DISABLED VETERAN SPECIAL REGISTRATION PLATES ISSUED BY THE
27 ADMINISTRATION.

28 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
29 October 1, 2026.
