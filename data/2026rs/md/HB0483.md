A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Business Regulation – Charitable Organizations – Audit and Review Thresholds

3 FOR the purpose of increasing the minimum gross income amounts from charitable
4 contributions at which a charitable organization must include a certain audit or a
5 certain review with the charitable organization’s registration statement; increasing
6 the amount of cash receipts from charitable contributions the Secretary of State may
7 accept an affidavit attesting to under certain circumstances; increasing the
8 maximum amount of gross income of a charitable organization at which the
9 Secretary of State may require an audit or review of the charitable organization; and
10 generally relating to audit and review thresholds for charitable organizations.
