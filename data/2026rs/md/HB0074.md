A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 State Procurement – Construction and Services – Contract Modification

3 FOR the purpose of requiring a certain procurement contract for construction or security
4 services to include a clause requiring contract modification for increased costs for
5 compensation and benefits under certain circumstances; requiring a certain contract
6 that is modified to be contingent on approval by the Chief Procurement Officer; and
7 generally relating to procurement contracts for construction and services.

8 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
9 Article – State Finance and Procurement
10 Section 13–218(b)
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
