A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Criminal Procedure – Expungement – Effect

3 FOR the purpose of establishing that a person who is granted an expungement shall be
4 determined for all purposes not to have been arrested for, cited for, charged with, or
5 convicted of the underlying offense; and generally relating to expungement.

6 BY adding to
7 Article – Criminal Procedure
8 Section 10–101.1
9 Annotated Code of Maryland
10 (2025 Replacement Volume)

11 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
12 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

13 Article – Criminal Procedure

14 10–101.1.

15 A PERSON WHO IS GRANTED AN EXPUNGEMENT UNDER THIS SUBTITLE SHALL
16 BE DETERMINED FOR ALL PURPOSES NOT TO HAVE BEEN ARRESTED FOR, CITED
17 FOR, CHARGED WITH, OR CONVICTED OF THE UNDERLYING OFFENSE.

18 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
19 October 1, 2026.
