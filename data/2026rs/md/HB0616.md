A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Health Services Cost Review Commission – Health Facilities – Jurisdiction and
3 Rate Setting

4 FOR the purpose of altering the jurisdiction of the Health Services Cost Review
5 Commission; requiring the Commission, in carrying out certain responsibilities
6 relating to evaluating facility resources to meet certain financial requirements and
7 reviewing costs and rates of hospital services, to take into account all costs incurred
8 or expenditures made by a health facility in connection with the operation of the
9 facility; and generally relating to health facility rate setting.
