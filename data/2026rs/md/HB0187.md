A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Criminal Procedure – Expungement – No Finding and Case Terminated Without
3 Finding

4 FOR the purpose of authorizing the expungement of a disposition under a certain provision
5 of law if the disposition was included in a case that was disposed of by the court with
6 a no finding designation or designated by the court as having been terminated
7 without finding; and generally relating to expungement.

3 (d) (1) If the person is convicted of a new crime during the applicable time
4 period set forth in subsection (c) of this section, the original conviction or convictions are
5 not eligible for expungement unless the new conviction becomes eligible for expungement.

6 (2) A person is not eligible for expungement if the person is a defendant in
7 a pending criminal proceeding.

8 (3) [If] SUBJECT TO SUBSECTION (J) OF THIS SECTION, IF a person is
9 not eligible for expungement of one conviction in a unit, the person is not eligible for
10 expungement of any other conviction in the unit.

11 (J) A DISPOSITION MAY BE EXPUNGED UNDER THIS SECTION IF THE CASE
12 WAS NOT DISPOSED OF BY AN UNEQUIVOCAL CONVICTION BUT WAS INSTEAD
13 INCLUDED IN A CASE THAT WAS:

14 (1) DISPOSED OF BY THE COURT WITH A NO FINDING DESIGNATION;
15 OR

16 (2) DESIGNATED BY THE COURT AS HAVING BEEN TERMINATED
17 WITHOUT FINDING.
