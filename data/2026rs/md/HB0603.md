1 (e) (1) For each taxable year, the property tax credit under this section is
2 calculated by:

19 (iii) for the municipal corporation property tax:

20 1. the homestead credit percentage established by the
21 municipal corporation under paragraph (4) of this subsection; or

22 2. if the municipal corporation has not set a percentage
23 under paragraph (4) of this subsection or has not notified the Department as required under
24 paragraph (7) of this subsection, the homestead credit percentage for the taxable year for
25 the county in which the property is located.

26 (3) Subject to paragraph (5) of this subsection, the Mayor and City Council
