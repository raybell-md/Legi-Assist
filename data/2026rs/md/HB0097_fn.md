State Designations - State Shark - Megalodon 
 
 
This bill designates the megalodon as the State shark. 
 
 
Fiscal Summary 
 
State Effect:  Designating a State shark does not materially affect State finances.     
  
Local Effect:  None.    
  
Small Business Effect:  None.  
  
 
Analysis 
 
Current Law:  Since the adoption of the Maryland State Flag in 1904 and the adoption of 
the Black-eyed Susan as the State flower in 1918, the State has adopted many official 
symbols, as detailed in Appendix – State Symbols of Maryland. The Governor is also 
authorized to designate a citizen as the State’s Poet Laureate. 
 
 
Additional Information 
 
Recent Prior Introductions:  Similar legislation has not been introduced in the last  
three years. 
 
Designated Cross File:  SB 135 (Senator Bailey) - Education, Energy, and the 
Environment.
