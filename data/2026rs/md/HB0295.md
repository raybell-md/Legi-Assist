A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Gaming – Prohibition on Interactive Games and Revenue From Illegal Markets

3 FOR the purpose of prohibiting certain persons from operating, conducting, or promoting
4 certain interactive games; requiring certain license applicants and licensees to report
5 to the State Lottery and Gaming Control Commission any business relationship with
6 certain persons known to support, operate, conduct, or promote certain interactive
7 games; requiring the Commission to deny certain license applications and revoke
8 certain licenses under certain circumstances; requiring certain license applicants
9 and licensees to submit a certain disclosure under certain circumstances; prohibiting
10 the Commission from issuing a license to certain persons or allowing the person to
11 commence or continue operations if the person or an affiliate of the person knowingly
12 accepts revenue that is directly or indirectly derived from certain jurisdictions;
13 authorizing the Commission to deny certain applications or impose certain sanctions
14 under certain circumstances; and generally relating to interactive games and illegal
15 gaming markets.

8 12–115.

9 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS

11 (2) “COMMISSION” MEANS THE STATE LOTTERY AND GAMING

13 (3) (I) “INTERACTIVE GAME” MEANS A GAME, CONTEST, OR
14 PROMOTION THAT:

15 1. IS AVAILABLE ON THE INTERNET OR ACCESSIBLE ON
16 A MOBILE PHONE, COMPUTER TERMINAL, OR SIMILAR DEVICE;

17 2. UTILIZES MULTIPLE CURRENCY SYSTEMS OF
18 PAYMENT ALLOWING THE PLAYER TO EXCHANGE THE CURRENCY FOR ANY PRIZE OR
19 AWARD OR CASH OR CASH EQUIVALENTS; AND

20 3. SIMULATES:

21 A. CASINO–STYLE GAMING, SUCH AS SLOT MACHINES,
22 VIDEO POKER, AND TABLE GAMES, INCLUDING BLACKJACK, ROULETTE, CRAPS, AND
23 POKER;

24 B. LOTTERY GAMES, INCLUDING DRAW GAMES, INSTANT
25 WIN GAMES, KENO, AND BINGO; OR

26 C. SPORTS WAGERING.

27 (II) “INTERACTIVE GAME” DOES NOT INCLUDE A GAME THAT
28 SOLELY AWARDS NONCASH PRIZES.
