L3 6lr2342

By: Delegates J. Long, Alston, and Korman
Introduced and read first time: January 21, 2026
Assigned to: Government, Labor, and Elections

A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Municipalities – Limiting Access to Beaches – Prohibition

3 FOR the purpose of prohibiting a municipality from limiting access to a certain area of a
4 beach to residents of the municipality; and generally relating to restricting access to
5 municipal beaches.

6 BY adding to
7 Article – Local Government
8 Section 4–112
9 Annotated Code of Maryland
10 (2013 Volume and 2025 Supplement)

11 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
12 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

13 Article – Local Government

14 4–112.

15 (A) THIS SECTION APPLIES ONLY TO A BEACH IN THE STATE THAT IS OWNED
16 BY A MUNICIPALITY.

17 (B) A MUNICIPALITY MAY NOT LIMIT ACCESS TO THE AREA OF A BEACH
18 THAT IS ABOVE THE MEAN HIGH TIDE, AFFECTED BY THE REGULAR RISE AND FALL
19 OF THE TIDE, SOLELY TO RESIDENTS OF THE MUNICIPALITY.

20 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
21 October 1, 2026.
