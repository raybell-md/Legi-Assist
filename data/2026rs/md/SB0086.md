A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 General Assembly – Office Space, Services, and Equipment

3 FOR the purpose of repealing the prohibition against a local government subsidizing a
4 member of the General Assembly in the maintenance of office space, services, or
5 equipment; and generally relating to office space, services, and equipment for
6 members of the General Assembly.

7 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
8 Article – State Government
9 Section 2–102
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

12 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
13 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

14 Article – State Government

15 2–102.

16 [(a)] The State shall provide the members of the General Assembly with reasonable
17 office space and services, equipment, and secretarial services, as provided in the State
18 budget.

19 [(b) (1) A municipal corporation, county, or other political subdivision or its
20 governing body may not subsidize a member of the General Assembly in the maintenance
21 of the space, services, or equipment.
