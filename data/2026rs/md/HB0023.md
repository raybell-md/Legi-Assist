A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Motor Vehicle Administration – Identification Cards – Issuance to Minors

3 FOR the purpose of prohibiting the Motor Vehicle Administration from requiring a parent
4 or legal guardian of a minor at least a certain age to cosign or be present at the time
5 of submission of the minor’s application for an identification card; requiring the
6 Administration to adopt regulations establishing acceptable forms of documentation
7 establishing proof of residency that a minor at least a certain age may use in an
8 application for an identification card; and generally relating to State–issued
9 identification cards for minors.

10 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
11 Article – Transportation
12 Section 12–301(a)
13 Annotated Code of Maryland
14 (2020 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
