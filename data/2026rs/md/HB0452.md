A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 State Board of Veterinary Medical Examiners – Grounds for Disciplinary Action
3 – Restrictions

4 FOR the purpose of prohibiting the State Board of Veterinary Medical Examiners from
5 suspending or revoking a license to practice veterinary medicine in the State,
6 reprimanding or censuring a licensee, or placing a licensee on probation solely on the
7 basis of the licensee discussing or recommending the use of cannabis or a product
8 that contains cannabidiol on an animal for certain purposes; and generally relating
9 to the State Board of Veterinary Medical Examiners and grounds for disciplinary
10 action.

1 Article – Agriculture

2 2–301.

3 (a) In this subtitle the following words have the meanings indicated.

4 (b) “Board” means the State Board of Veterinary Medical Examiners.

5 (d) “License” means a license to practice veterinary medicine in the State.

6 (h) “Veterinarian” means any person who is a graduate of a college of veterinary
7 medicine.

8 (i) “Veterinary practitioner” means a licensed and registered veterinarian
9 engaged in the practice of veterinary medicine.

10 2–310.

11 (A) [The] SUBJECT TO SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS SECTION, THE Board may
12 refuse, suspend, or revoke any application or license, and censure or place on probation any
13 licensee after a hearing, if the veterinarian or veterinary practitioner:

14 (1) Is unable to practice veterinary medicine competently due to a physical
15 or mental disability;

16 (2) Is convicted of a violation of any federal or State law relating to
17 prescription drugs, a controlled dangerous substance under Title 5, Subtitle 4 of the
18 Criminal Law Article, or a controlled substance as defined by 21 U.S.C. § 812;

19 (3) Is convicted of a felony, or of a crime involving moral turpitude;

20 (4) Is convicted of violating any provision of this subtitle, any unlawful or
21 fraudulent practice, or any fraudulent, misleading, or deceptive representation or
22 advertising concerning his professional qualifications or the quality of materials or drugs
23 used by him in his professional work or in the treatment of animals;

24 (5) Has a final judgment entered against him in a civil malpractice case
25 involving gross personal negligence;

26 (6) Obtains the license by fraud or misrepresentation, either in the
27 application, or in passing the examination;

28 (7) Is guilty of employing or permitting to practice veterinary medicine any
29 person who does not hold a license to practice veterinary medicine in the State;

30 (8) Fails to comply with Board rules or regulations after receiving a license;

1 (9) Is grossly negligent or deliberately cruel to an animal;

2 (10) Is determined by four members to be professionally incompetent as a
3 veterinary practitioner;

4 (11) Is disciplined by a licensing authority of another state, including the
5 suspension or revocation of a license to practice veterinary medicine, for an act that would

10 subtitle.

11 (B) THE BOARD MAY NOT SUSPEND OR REVOKE A LICENSE, REPRIMAND OR
12 CENSURE A LICENSEE, OR PLACE A LICENSEE ON PROBATION SOLELY ON THE BASIS
13 OF THE LICENSEE DISCUSSING OR RECOMMENDING THE USE OF CANNABIS, AS
14 DEFINED IN § 1–101 OF THE ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES AND CANNABIS ARTICLE, OR
15 A PRODUCT THAT CONTAINS CANNABIDIOL ON AN ANIMAL FOR POTENTIAL
16 THERAPEUTIC EFFECT OR HEALTH SUPPLEMENTATION PURPOSES.

17 Article – Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis

18 1–101.

19 (a) In this article the following words have the meanings indicated.

20 (d) (1) “Cannabis” means the plant Cannabis sativa L. and any part of the
21 plant, including all derivatives, extracts, cannabinoids, isomers, acids, salts, and salts of
22 isomers, whether growing or not, with a delta–9–tetrahydrocannabinol concentration
23 greater than 0.3% on a dry weight basis.

24 (2) “Cannabis” includes cannabis products, seeds, seedlings, immature
25 plants, and clones.

26 (3) “Cannabis” does not include hemp or hemp products, as defined in §
27 14–101 of the Agriculture Article.

28 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
