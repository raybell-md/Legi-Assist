A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Department of Natural Resources – Licenses, Permits, Registrations, and Public
3 Profiles – Notation of Veteran Status

4 FOR the purpose of authorizing the Department of Natural Resources to establish a certain
5 process to include a notation of veteran status on a license, permit, registration, or
6 public profile; and generally relating to veterans and licenses, permits, registrations,
7 and public profiles of the Department of Natural Resources.

8 BY adding to
9 Article – Natural Resources
10 Section 1–403.2
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2023 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

15 Article – Natural Resources

16 1–403.2.

17 (A) THE DEPARTMENT MAY ESTABLISH A PROCESS TO INCLUDE A
18 NOTATION INDICATING VETERAN STATUS ON A LICENSE, PERMIT, REGISTRATION,
19 OR PUBLIC PROFILE.

20 (B) IF THE DEPARTMENT ESTABLISHES A PROCESS UNDER THIS SECTION,
21 AN APPLICATION FOR A LICENSE, PERMIT, OR REGISTRATION SHALL ALLOW THE
22 APPLICANT TO REQUEST THAT A NOTATION OF THE APPLICANT’S VETERAN STATUS
23 BE INCLUDED ON THE LICENSE, PERMIT, REGISTRATION, OR PUBLIC PROFILE.

1 (C) AN APPLICANT FOR A LICENSE, PERMIT, OR REGISTRATION WHO
2 REQUESTS THAT A NOTATION OF THE APPLICANT’S VETERAN STATUS BE INCLUDED
3 ON THE LICENSE, PERMIT, REGISTRATION, OR PUBLIC PROFILE SHALL:

4 (1) SUBMIT TO THE DEPARTMENT ONE OF THE FOLLOWING:

5 (I) A CERTIFICATION OF VETERAN STATUS OBTAINED FROM
6 THE DEPARTMENT OF VETERANS AND MILITARY FAMILIES IN ACCORDANCE WITH §
7 9–905 OF THE STATE GOVERNMENT ARTICLE;

8 (II) A FEDERAL CERTIFICATE OF RELEASE OR DISCHARGE
9 FROM ACTIVE DUTY (DD FORM 214); OR

10 (III) ANY OTHER DOCUMENTATION CERTIFYING VETERAN
11 STATUS ACCEPTABLE TO THE DEPARTMENT; AND

12 (2) INDICATE THAT THE APPLICANT CONSENTS TO:

13 (I) HAVING A NOTATION ON THE APPLICANT’S LICENSE,
14 PERMIT, REGISTRATION, OR PUBLIC PROFILE INDICATING THAT THE APPLICANT IS
15 A VETERAN; AND

16 (II) BEING CONTACTED BY APPROPRIATE EXECUTIVE BRANCH
17 AGENCIES REGARDING THE APPLICANT’S ELIGIBILITY FOR STATE OR FEDERAL
18 VETERANS’ BENEFITS.

19 (D) IF AN APPLICANT SUBMITS THE DOCUMENTATION REQUIRED UNDER
20 SUBSECTION (C)(1) OF THIS SECTION AND CONSENTS TO BEING CONTACTED UNDER
21 SUBSECTION (C)(2) OF THIS SECTION, THE DEPARTMENT SHALL INCLUDE A
22 NOTATION OF VETERAN STATUS ON THE LICENSE, PERMIT, REGISTRATION, OR
23 PUBLIC PROFILE.

24 (E) IN ACCORDANCE WITH §§ 4–304 THROUGH 4–325 OF THE GENERAL
25 PROVISIONS ARTICLE AND ANY OTHER APPLICABLE LAW, AND ON REQUEST BY AN
26 EXECUTIVE BRANCH AGENCY, THE DEPARTMENT SHALL ELECTRONICALLY
27 TRANSMIT TO THE EXECUTIVE BRANCH AGENCY APPROPRIATE INFORMATION
28 ABOUT EACH LICENSE HOLDER, PERMIT HOLDER, REGISTRANT, OR INDIVIDUAL
29 WITH A PUBLIC PROFILE WHO HAS CONSENTED TO BEING CONTACTED UNDER
30 SUBSECTION (C)(2) OF THIS SECTION.

31 (F) THE DEPARTMENT MAY ADOPT REGULATIONS TO CARRY OUT THIS
32 SECTION.
