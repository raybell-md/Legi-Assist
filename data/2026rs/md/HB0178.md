A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Public Health – Sickle Cell Disease – Specialized Clinics and Scholarship
3 Program for Medical Residents

4 FOR the purpose of requiring the Maryland Department of Health to establish certain
5 specialized clinics for the management and treatment of sickle cell disease and
6 establish a scholarship program for medical residents who specialize in classical
7 hematology with a focus on sickle cell disease; and generally relating to sickle cell
8 disease.

9 BY adding to
10 Article – Health – General
11 Section 18–510 and 18–511
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2023 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

14 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
15 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

16 Article – Health – General

17 18–510.

18 (A) (1) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL ESTABLISH THREE SPECIALIZED
19 CLINICS DEDICATED TO THE MANAGEMENT AND TREATMENT OF SICKLE CELL
20 DISEASE.

21 (2) THE CLINICS ESTABLISHED UNDER PARAGRAPH (1) OF THIS
22 SUBSECTION SHALL BE LOCATED IN:

1 (I) MONTGOMERY COUNTY;

2 (II) HARFORD COUNTY; AND

3 (III) A COUNTY LOCATED ON THE EASTERN SHORE.

4 (B) EACH CLINIC ESTABLISHED UNDER SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION
5 SHALL:

6 (1) OPERATE UNDER A HUB–AND–SPOKE MODEL WITH A
7 SPECIALIZED SICKLE CELL DISEASE CARE HUB;

8 (2) PROVIDE ROUTINE CARE, PAIN MANAGEMENT, GENETIC
9 COUNSELING, MENTAL HEALTH SERVICES, SURVEILLANCE, AND PATIENT
10 EDUCATION; AND

11 (3) IN COLLABORATION WITH THE SPECIALIZED SICKLE CELL
12 DISEASE CARE HUB:

13 (I) ENSURE CONSISTENT ACCESS TO HUB–BASED SICKLE CELL
14 DISEASE SPECIALISTS THROUGH TELEHEALTH OR ON–SITE VISITS TO MANAGE
15 COMPLEX PATIENT NEEDS;

16 (II) OFFER COMPREHENSIVE BEHAVIORAL HEALTH AND
17 SOCIAL SUPPORT SERVICES TO ADDRESS THE MULTIFACETED CHALLENGES FACED
18 BY INDIVIDUALS WITH SICKLE CELL DISEASE;

19 (III) OPERATE OR COORDINATE INFUSION THERAPY AND
20 STRUCTURED PAIN MANAGEMENT PROGRAMS TAILORED TO THE CLINICAL
21 DEMANDS OF SICKLE CELL DISEASE PATIENTS;

22 (IV) COLLABORATE WITH LOCAL PRIMARY CARE PROVIDERS
23 AND SOCIAL SERVICES AGENCIES TO STREAMLINE REFERRALS, REDUCE CARE GAPS,
24 AND ADDRESS SOCIAL DETERMINANTS OF HEALTH WITHIN THE COMMUNITY; AND

25 (V) PARTNER WITH A SICKLE CELL DISEASE–FOCUSED,
26 COMMUNITY–BASED ORGANIZATION LOCATED IN THE STATE TO IDENTIFY AND
27 ADDRESS SOCIAL BARRIERS, INCLUDING ASSISTANCE WITH TRANSPORTATION,
28 HOUSING, NUTRITION, AND OTHER ESSENTIAL SUPPORTS.

29 (C) (1) FOR FISCAL YEAR 2028, THE GOVERNOR SHALL INCLUDE IN THE
30 ANNUAL BUDGET BILL AN APPROPRIATION OF $6,138,000 TO SUPPORT CLINIC
31 OPERATIONS, STAFFING, TRAINING, AND SOCIAL SUPPORT SERVICES.

9 (A) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL ESTABLISH A SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM FOR

13 THIS SECTION SHALL PROVIDE FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE TO THE MEDICAL RESIDENT
