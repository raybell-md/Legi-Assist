A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 State Transfer Tax – Land Preservation Special Fund – Revenue Allocation

3 FOR the purpose of repealing a requirement that for certain fiscal years a certain amount
4 of the balance of a certain special fund dedicated to certain land preservation
5 programs be allocated to the General Fund of the State; and generally relating to the
6 use of a certain special fund dedicated to certain land preservation programs.

17 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
18 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

19 Article – Tax – Property

20 13–209.

21 (a) (1) Before any other distribution under this section, in any fiscal year that
22 bonds secured by a pledge of the State transfer tax are outstanding, the revenue from the
23 transfer tax shall be used to pay, as and when due, the principal of and interest on the
24 bonds.
