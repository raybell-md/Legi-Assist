16 Preamble

17 WHEREAS, Maryland has been without an official State song since the repeal of
18 “Maryland, My Maryland” in 2021; and

21 WHEREAS, The people of Maryland take great pride in the State’s rich history,

1 WHEREAS, Music and poetry have long served as expressions of a state’s history,
2 spirit, and unity, offering a sense of pride and belonging to the people it represents; and

3 WHEREAS, “The Heart of Maryland”, composed by Jayla Elise, beautifully
4 expresses the pride, warmth, and unity of the State and its people; and

5 WHEREAS, “The Heart of Maryland” celebrates the State’s beauty, resilience, and
6 diversity, capturing the warmth and strength of Maryland’s people from the mountains of
7 the west to the shores of the Chesapeake Bay and beyond; and

8 WHEREAS, The song honors Maryland’s rich cultural and historical heritage while
9 embracing the inclusivity, compassion, and progress that define the State today; and

10 WHEREAS, Marylanders have long sought a unifying State song that all residents
11 can sing with pride, regardless of background, and that reflects the true heart and
12 character of Maryland; and

13 WHEREAS, “The Heart of Maryland” reflects a deep appreciation of Maryland’s
14 landscapes, communities, and shared traditions, offering an inclusive and uplifting anthem
15 for all Marylanders; and

16 WHEREAS, The adoption of “The Heart of Maryland” as the official State song
17 honors Maryland’s enduring spirit and affirms the values of unity, diversity, and pride in
18 the State’s shared future; now, therefore,

19 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
20 That Section(s) 7–319 and 7–320 of Article – General Provisions of the Annotated Code of
21 Maryland be renumbered to be Section(s) 7–320 and 7–321, respectively.

22 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That the Laws of Maryland read
23 as follows:

24 Article – General Provisions

25 7–319.

26 (A) THE SONG “THE HEART OF MARYLAND”, WRITTEN BY JAYLA ELISE
27 FROM DORCHESTER COUNTY FOR THE MARYLAND 250 PROJECT, IS THE STATE
28 SONG.
