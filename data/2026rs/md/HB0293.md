A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Maryland Longitudinal Data System Center – External Data Sharing With
3 Third–Party Data Centers for Multistate Reporting – Authorization

4 FOR the purpose of authorizing the Governing Board of the Maryland Longitudinal Data
5 System Center to provide certain student and workforce data to a third–party data
6 center under certain circumstances; requiring each third–party data center to agree
7 to comply with certain requirements relating to the data the center receives;
8 requiring the Center to enter into a written data sharing agreement with certain
9 third–party data centers; repealing the Governing Board’s power to authorize the
10 sharing of data with the U.S. Census Bureau under certain circumstances; and
11 generally relating to the authority of the Maryland Longitudinal Data System
12 Center to share data externally.
