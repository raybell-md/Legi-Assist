A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Board of Public Works – Contract Review – Climate and Sustainability
3 Information

4 FOR the purpose of requiring a State agency to provide certain climate and sustainability
5 information on submission of a certain contract award recommendation to the Board
6 of Public Works; requiring certain liaisons to the Board to ensure compliance with
7 this Act; requiring the climate and sustainability information to be placed on the
8 Board agenda; and generally relating to the Board of Public Works and climate and
9 sustainability.

1 10–208.

2 (A) THIS SECTION APPLIES TO THE FOLLOWING CATEGORIES OF
3 CONTRACTS SUBMITTED TO THE BOARD FOR ITS APPROVAL:

4 (1) ARCHITECTURAL AND ENGINEERING SERVICES;

5 (2) CONSTRUCTION;

6 (3) ENERGY PERFORMANCE;

7 (4) GENERAL MISCELLANEOUS;

8 (5) LANDLORD LEASE; AND

9 (6) TENANT LEASE.

10 (B) THE BOARD SHALL REQUIRE AN AGENCY TO PROVIDE, TO THE EXTENT
11 APPLICABLE, THE FOLLOWING CLIMATE AND SUSTAINABILITY INFORMATION ON
12 SUBMISSION OF A CONTRACT AWARD RECOMMENDATION TO THE BOARD:

13 (1) IF THE CONTRACT CONTRIBUTES TO THE STATE’S GREENHOUSE
14 GAS EMISSIONS REDUCTION OR NET–ZERO CARBON GOALS, HOW THOSE BENEFITS
15 ARE ATTAINED;

16 (2) HOW THE CONTRACT PROVIDES FOR RESILIENCE ADAPTATION TO
17 PROJECTED CLIMATE CHANGE AND EXTREME WEATHER EVENTS OVER THE TERM OR
18 THE USEFUL LIFE OF THE SUPPLIES OR SERVICES;

19 (3) HOW THE CONTRACT ADVANCES THE STATE’S GOALS AND
20 LEADERSHIP ON CLIMATE MITIGATION, ADAPTATION, RESILIENCY, OR INNOVATION
21 FOR SUSTAINABLY SOURCED SUPPLIES OR SERVICES;

22 (4) HOW THE CONTRACT INCLUDES SUPPLIES OR SERVICES THAT ARE
23 ENVIRONMENTALLY PREFERABLE, AS DEFINED BY THE MARYLAND GREEN
24 PURCHASING COMMITTEE UNDER § 14–410 OF THIS ARTICLE;

25 (5) HOW THE CONTRACT INCORPORATES ASPECTS OF DESIGN OR
26 CONSTRUCTION THAT ARE CONSISTENT WITH THE STANDARDS ADOPTED BY THE
27 MARYLAND GREEN BUILDING COUNCIL; AND
