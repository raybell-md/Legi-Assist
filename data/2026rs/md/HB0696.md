A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 State Prescription Drug Benefits – Retirees

3 FOR the purpose of authorizing certain retirees who began State service before a certain
4 date to be eligible for prescription drug benefits in the State Employee and Retiree
5 Health and Welfare Benefits Program; specifying that prescription drug benefits
6 shall be discontinued for certain Medicare–eligible retirees who began State service
7 on or after a certain date and their Medicare–eligible spouses and dependent
8 children; repealing the Maryland State Retiree Prescription Drug Coverage
9 Program, the Maryland State Retiree Catastrophic Prescription Drug Assistance
10 Program, and the Maryland State Retiree Life–Sustaining Prescription Drug
11 Assistance Program; repealing a requirement that the Secretary of Budget and
12 Management provide a certain notice; repealing a requirement for the Department
13 of Budget and Management to submit certain reports and ensure Medicare–eligible
14 retirees have access to certain services; and generally relating to State prescription
15 drug benefits for retirees from State employment.
