SENATE BILL 310

E2 6lr2595

By: Senator Jennings
Introduced and read first time: January 21, 2026
Assigned to: Judicial Proceedings

A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Criminal Records – Maryland Judiciary Case Search – Traffic Violation by a
3 Minor

4 FOR the purpose of prohibiting the Maryland Judiciary Case Search from in any way
5 referring to the existence of records relating to an alleged violation of the Maryland
6 Vehicle Law or any other traffic law, ordinance, or regulation by a minor; and
7 generally relating to criminal records.

8 BY adding to
9 Article – Criminal Procedure
10 Section 10–402
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2025 Replacement Volume)

13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

15 Article – Criminal Procedure

16 10–402.

17 THE MARYLAND JUDICIARY CASE SEARCH MAY NOT IN ANY WAY REFER TO
18 THE EXISTENCE OF RECORDS RELATING TO AN ALLEGED VIOLATION OF THE
19 MARYLAND VEHICLE LAW OR ANY OTHER TRAFFIC LAW, ORDINANCE, OR
20 REGULATION BY A MINOR.

21 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
22 October 1, 2026.
