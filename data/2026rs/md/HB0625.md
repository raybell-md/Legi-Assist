2 HOUSE BILL 625

1 SUBTITLE 25. HUMAN–RELEVANT RESEARCH FUND CONTRIBUTION COLLECTION.

2 2–2501.

3 IN THIS SUBTITLE, “ANIMAL AND PLANT HEALTH INSPECTION SERVICE
4 FORM 7023” MEANS THE FORM REQUIRED TO BE SUBMITTED TO THE FEDERAL
5 DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE UNDER 7 U.S.C. § 2143 AND 9 C.F.R. § 2.36.

6 2–2502.

7 (A) ON OR BEFORE JANUARY 15 EACH YEAR, BEGINNING IN 2024, EACH
8 RESEARCH FACILITY THAT IS LOCATED IN THE STATE AND IS REQUIRED TO SUBMIT
9 AN ANIMAL AND PLANT HEALTH INSPECTION SERVICE FORM 7023 SHALL PAY TO
10 THE DEPARTMENT A CONTRIBUTION IN THE AMOUNT OF:

11 (1) $5,000 FOR UP TO 100 ANIMALS;

12 (2) $10,000 FOR MORE THAN 100 BUT NOT MORE THAN 500 ANIMALS;

13 (3) $55,000 FOR MORE THAN 500 BUT NOT MORE THAN 5,000
14 ANIMALS; AND

15 (4) $75,000 FOR MORE THAN 5,000 ANIMALS.

16 (B) THE AMOUNT OF THE CONTRIBUTION TO BE PAID UNDER SUBSECTION
17 (A) OF THIS SECTION SHALL BE BASED ON THE TOTAL NUMBER OF ANIMALS
18 REPORTED IN COLUMN F ON THE MOST RECENTLY SUBMITTED ANIMAL AND PLANT
19 HEALTH INSPECTION SERVICE FORM 7023.

20 (C) THE SECRETARY SHALL DISTRIBUTE THE FUNDS PAID UNDER
21 SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION TO THE HUMAN–RELEVANT RESEARCH FUND
22 ESTABLISHED UNDER § 10–4D–02 OF THE ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT ARTICLE.

23 2–2503.

24 A RESEARCH FACILITY THAT FAILS TO PAY THE CONTRIBUTION REQUIRED
25 UNDER § 2–2502(A) OF THIS SUBTITLE MAY BE SUBJECT TO A CIVIL PENALTY OF NOT
26 MORE THAN $1,000 PER DAY.

27 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
28 October 1, 2026.
